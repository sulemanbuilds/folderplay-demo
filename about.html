Your music.
No cloud required.

FolderPlay is a lightweight, privacy-first music player that runs entirely in your browser. Drop a folder, press play — that's it.

Open Player

100% Private

Your music never leaves your device. No uploads, no accounts, no tracking. Files are read directly in the browser using the Web File API.

Instant Playback

Select a folder and your entire library loads immediately. No waiting for uploads or encoding — playback starts the moment you click.

Format Friendly

Plays any audio format your browser supports — MP3, FLAC, WAV, OGG, AAC, and more. Handles entire music libraries with ease.

Full Controls

Seekable progress bar, drag-to-seek, volume control with mute, shuffle, repeat, next/previous navigation, and active song highlighting.

Mobile Ready

Fully responsive design that works great on phones and tablets. Touch-friendly controls and a layout that adapts to any screen size.

Pure Web Tech

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — zero dependencies, zero frameworks. Fast, lightweight, and easy to understand or modify.
